Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2025

Key Point: Stocksnack is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our automated content posting service.

1. Introduction

Stocksnack ("we," "our," or "us") operates an automated workflow application that integrates with social media platforms including TikTok. This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive authentication tokens to post content on your behalf
  • Configuration Data: Settings and preferences for your automated workflows
  • Content Data: Information used to generate and post content (stock symbols, market data preferences)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our service (workflow executions, API calls)
  • Log Data: Technical information such as IP addresses, timestamps, and error logs
  • API Response Data: Data returned from TikTok and other integrated platforms

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • Financial Data: Stock market information from Yahoo Finance, Financial Modeling Prep, and other data providers
  • Social Media Data: Information from TikTok, YouTube, and other connected platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our automated posting service
  • Execute workflows and post content to your social media accounts
  • Analyze stock market data and generate financial content
  • Monitor and improve our service performance
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Communicate with you about service updates and issues

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We share information with third-party services necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok: Content, videos, and metadata for posting
  • YouTube: Videos and metadata for uploads
  • Financial Data Providers: API requests for stock market data
  • Cloud Services: Data storage and processing (if applicable)

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

4.3 Service Providers

We may share information with service providers who help us operate our service, subject to confidentiality obligations.

5. TikTok Integration

When you connect your TikTok account to Stocksnack:

  • We receive OAuth access tokens to post content on your behalf
  • We access only the permissions you explicitly grant
  • Your TikTok data is subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • You can revoke our access at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • We do not store your TikTok password

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

When you disconnect your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within 90 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication tokens storage
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and logging

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to access and update your information.

8.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by disconnecting your accounts or contacting us.

8.3 Revoke Access

You can revoke Stocksnack's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok settings.

8.4 Opt-Out

You can stop using our service at any time by discontinuing your workflows.

9. Children's Privacy

Stocksnack is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (if applicable)
  • Posting a notice in our application

Your continued use of Stocksnack after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right of access, correction, and deletion
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities
